Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how this application ("App") handles information when you use it to publish video content to TikTok and other social media platforms. This is a personal automation tool and does not collect or sell user data.

2. Information We Access

To function, this App accesses the following through the TikTok API:

  • TikTok account information: username and account ID, used solely to identify the account for publishing
  • Video upload permissions: used to post video content to TikTok on your behalf

No personal data beyond what is required for API functionality is accessed or stored.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information accessed through the TikTok API is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate and authorize video uploads to your TikTok account
  • Confirm successful publication of content

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

4. Data Storage

API access tokens are stored locally on the owner's private server and are never transmitted to external services beyond TikTok's own API endpoints. No user data is stored in external databases.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as necessary to operate the automation. You may revoke access at any time via your TikTok account settings under "Manage app permissions".

6. Third-Party Services

This App integrates with the TikTok API. TikTok's own privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy available at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke this App's access to your TikTok account at any time
  • Request deletion of any locally stored tokens

8. Security

API credentials are stored in a private, access-controlled server environment. We take reasonable precautions to protect this information from unauthorized access.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

For questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the App owner directly.
